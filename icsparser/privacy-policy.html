Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

ICSParser ("the App") is developed by BelousovAV. This policy describes how we handle your information.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App operates entirely on your device.

Calendar Data

The App accesses your device calendars solely to import events from ICS files you provide. Calendar data is processed locally and is never sent to any server or third party.

File Access

ICS files you open with the App are read locally to parse calendar events. Files are not uploaded, copied, or shared externally.

Analytics & Tracking

The App does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools. We do not track your usage or behavior.

Subscriptions

In-app subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Subscription status is verified on-device using Apple's StoreKit framework.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
