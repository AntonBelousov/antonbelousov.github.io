ICSParser Support

Common Questions

How do I import a calendar file?
Open any .ics file from Mail, Files, Safari, or other apps. Choose "Open in ICSParser" from the share sheet. Preview events, select a calendar, and tap Import.

What file formats are supported?
ICS (.ics), iCalendar (.ical, .icalendar), and Free/Busy (.ifb) files.

The app says "duplicate found" — what does that mean?
The app detected an event that already exists in your calendar. You can skip it, update the existing one, or choose for each event individually. You can set a default behavior in Settings.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Open Settings on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then Subscriptions. Find ICSParser and tap Cancel Subscription.

Is my data safe?
Yes. All data is processed on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. See our Privacy Policy.
