3. Information We Collect

The App is primarily designed to run locally on your device and does not require account registration.

Depending on your configuration and platform settings, we may process the following categories of data:

3.1. Data you provide

  • In-app configuration and preferences (for example: saved ranges, custom settings, training configuration)

This data is stored locally on your device and is used only to provide the App’s functionality.

3.2. Purchase and subscription data

If you purchase a subscription or other in-app products, the transaction is processed by Apple via App Store / StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment details (such as card number).

We may have access to limited information provided by Apple for the purposes of:

  • Validating your purchase or subscription status
  • Enabling or disabling premium features in the App

This information may include an anonymized identifier, product identifier, and subscription status (active, expired, and similar).

3.3. Analytics and diagnostics (if enabled)

The App itself does not require analytics to function. However, we may use privacy-friendly analytics or crash-reporting tools (for example, via Apple or other providers) to understand usage patterns and improve stability.

This typically includes:

  • App version and device model
  • Operating system version
  • Anonymous usage events (for example, feature usage counts)
  • Crash logs

We do not attempt to identify you personally based on this information.

We use information only for the following purposes:

We do not sell your personal data.

You can delete local App data at any time by:
