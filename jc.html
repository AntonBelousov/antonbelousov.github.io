Your game data is protected by your device's operating system. Because we do not receive or process personal data, there is no risk of personal data leakage from our servers.

All App data resides only on your device. To delete your data, delete the App from your device. Deleted local data cannot be restored by us.

No international data transfers occur because we do not collect or transmit personal data.
